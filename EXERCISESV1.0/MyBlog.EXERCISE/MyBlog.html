What are Microgreens?

Microgreens are the infant stage of the regular matured vegetables found in our daily grocery and produces. They are usually harvested from 7-21 days after harvesting and come with a true plethora of benefits.

How are they grown?

Microgreens can be grown in soil, or hydroponically.They typically require little maintenance but can be prone to fungal or bacterial attacks due to young age. They are usually harvested from coco coir mixtures and offer several more nutrional, environmental, and financial benefits not just to the everyday person but to a nation or even our whold world.

Microgreens v. Matured Greens

Microgreens have 4x to 40x the nutrional/vitamin/mineral content of their matured counterparts.
